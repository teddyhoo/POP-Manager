January 19, 2018

MANUFACTURER

DISTRIBUTION

11 Just Food for Dogs retail locations

 The green bean ingredient in the listed products may be contaminated with Listeria monocytogenes. Although symptoms are generally mild (vomiting, diarrhea), more serious symptoms may develop (fever, impaired muscular and respiratory function). Humans may contract it by coming into contact with contaminated feces.
